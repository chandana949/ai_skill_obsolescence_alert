# SkillGuard

SkillGuard is an AI-powered system that helps blue-collar and mid-skill workers understand their risk of automation and receive personalized guidance to future-proof their careers.

---

## Problem Statement
Rapid automation and AI adoption are making many traditional job skills obsolete. Most workers lack timely awareness and clear guidance on which skills are at risk and how to upskill effectively.

---

## Solution Overview
SkillGuard analyzes a worker’s job role and skills to:
- Assess automation and skill obsolescence risk
- Provide AI-driven explanations for the risk
- Recommend future-ready skills and learning paths
- Offer interactive career guidance through AI chat

---

## Key Features
- Role-based automation risk assessment  
- AI-powered career and skill guidance  
- Personalized upskilling recommendations  
- Interactive AI career advisor  

---

## Google Technologies Used
- **Google AI Studio** – Access and management of Gemini models  
- **Gemini API (Gemini Pro)** – Core AI engine for analysis and recommendations  
- **Official Google Generative AI SDK (`@google/generative-ai`)**

---

## AI Explainability
While the underlying Gemini model is a black-box foundation model, SkillGuard ensures transparency by providing clear reasons and actionable insights for every prediction and recommendation.
